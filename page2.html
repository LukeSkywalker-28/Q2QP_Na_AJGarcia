With about 29% of Africa’s population lacking access to clean water, this increases the risk of many life-threatening diseases. With about 29% of Africa’s population lacking access to clean water, this increases the risk of many life-threatening diseases.

Our campaign aims to take action by sending our heartfilled volunteers to the sparse continent of Africa. Partnering with local governments, and hiking to multiple remote villages to give people the resources that they desperately need.

Ongoing Activities:

  • Delivery of food and water
  • Construction of small wells
  • Building linkage paths in small bodies of water

Future Activities:

  • Construction of water pumps, to start in mid-2024
  • Promoting education amongst the youth about water salinization and hygiene, planned to start in February 2024
