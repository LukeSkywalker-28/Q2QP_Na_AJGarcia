Welcome to the official website of IWAIAC! or Inclusive Water Access In Africa Campaign. An organization that started in 2020 promoting the inclusion of clean water and sanitation to the continent of Africa.

Our activities include: building wells, faucets and waterways guided by highly-ranked engineers from high-end affirms. Become part of the new frontier of inspiration and change!
